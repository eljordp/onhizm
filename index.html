The Meaning

More Than a Name.
A Feeling.

ONHIZM is a word created by Warsh — inspired by John Koenig's onism, the frustration of being stuck in one body that inhabits only one place at a time. A feeling of disconnection, of solitude you didn't choose.

This is clothing for the ones who feel it.
