The Origin

ONHIZM is a word Warsh created. It comes from onism — the frustration of being stuck in one body, one place, at a time.

Warsh took it further. His version of onism is about feeling cut off from everything around you. Sitting alone in your head even when you don't want to be.

ONHIZM is the clothing that carries that weight. Made for the ones who feel it. The thinkers. The ones who move different because they see different.
